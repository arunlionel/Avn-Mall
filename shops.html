The best shopping in Miami awaits at Aventura Mall. A true fashion lover’s paradise, the luxury shopping mall offers a lineup of the world’s most coveted brands, including Hermès, Balenciaga, Gucci, BVLGARI, Louis Vuitton, Cartier, Valentino, Fendi, Givenchy, Burberry, Saint Laurent, Tiffany & Co., Rolex, and more.

For a full suite of personalized fashion services, experience the Aventura Mall’s Stylist program. Access image consultations, wardrobe development, gift procurement and more in an elegant and exclusive setting.

Blurring the lines between interior and exterior design, Aventura Mall is highlighted by a two-level Apple store, as well as five department stores – including Nordstrom, Bloomingdale’s and Macy’s – and 300+ highly sought-after retailers. Shop at Aritzia, Anthropologie, Alo Yoga, Charlotte Tilbury, Lacoste, Nike, Ralph Lauren, Sephora, SKIMS and a two-level Zara.
