THE STYLIST @ AVENTURA MALL

Tired of sifting through myriad of summer trends that would make sense in your wardrobe? Does figuring out what to wear this fall leave you hyperventilating? Should you invest in Hermes’ Birkin or is Louis Vuitton’s Bella Tote more your style? Not sure where to start? Book an appointment with Aventura Mall’s Stylist today to enjoy unparalleled access to the very best the mall has to offer. With maximum convenience and unprecedented access, our relationship-building service provides a discreet and intimate experience while catering to the needs of our most discerning and luxury-minded guests.


KIMMIE SUSSMAN:Empowering
Your Style

Bringing 18 years of fashion experience helping such clients as Serena Williams, A Rod and The Marleys, Kimberly Sussman’s work has been featured in campaigns and runways throughout the world. Kimmie deeply understands the essence of style and will provide one-on-one fashion advice to help capture the looks you love. Whether your signature style is sophisticated and striking, casual yet voguish, tailored and crisp – or anything else – Kimmie makes wardrobe magic happen.





Style In Comfort

Your exclusive experience will take place in the privacy of our Stylist Suite located on the third level of the Nordstrom Wing, where your capsule edit sourced directly from the shops at Aventura Mall will await your arrival. Peruse your choices displayed in our elegant and sophisticated atelier, where our Stylists will dazzle you with the latest arsenal of “It” bags, enviable jewelry, and statement-making pieces. Whether you are seeking the latest in luxury from brands including Gucci, Burberry and Givenchy or you would like to refresh your vacation wardrobe from brands including BLENDED, Maje, Sandro, Michael Kors, Tory Burch or you are looking to step up your footwear game with the likes of Jimmy Choo, Valentino, Christian Louboutin or Stuart Weitzman The Stylist is here to elevate your shopping experience into an effortless pursuit of refinement, from our warm reception to our cordial farewell.
