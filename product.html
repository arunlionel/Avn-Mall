Anytime is a good time to drop by Treats Food Hall, where palate-pleasing plates and scrumptious bites are the order of the day! Enjoy the tastiest global cuisines, from Caribbean-inspired bowls at Jrk! and Yalla Motek’s fresh Mediterranean flavors, to authentic Old World recipes at Villa Italian Kitchen, Yip’s delicious dim sum – and just about anything else you may be craving! Sit outdoors in the shaded patio, or indoors, enjoying the lovely views, ample natural light and community seating, which includes shared banquettes and bar tables. With its immersive atmosphere and broad international offerings, Treats Food Hall is a treat for all the senses.
