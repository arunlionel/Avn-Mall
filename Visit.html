Ideally situated between Miami and Fort Lauderdale, Aventura Mall is easily accessible from South Florida’s major highways and is close to the region’s international airports and cruise ports. A perfect way to spend an entire day (or a few hours), the world-renowned shopping destination includes department stores such as Nordstrom, Bloomingdale’s and Macy’s, as well as 300+ boutiques and shopper favorites – from Gucci, Louis Vuitton, Ferragamo, Balenciaga, Hermès and Givenchy to Aritzia, Apple, Alo Yoga, Lacoste, Nike and Zara.

When you need a break from shopping, Aventura Mall also features a diverse array of chef-driven restaurants and eclectic eateries that will tempt every palate. Looking for a quick bite to eat? Treats Food Hall offers an array of options and seating in both a sun-filled, indoor space and a shaded rooftop area. Culture seekers can enjoy a self-guided audio tour of the Arts Aventura Mall program, which includes 25+ pieces located throughout the shopping center, highlighted by Carsten Höller’s 93-foot-tall Aventura Slide Tower, The Haas Brothers’ Gorillas in the Mist and a LOVE sculpture by Robert Indiana – the ideal spot for Instagram shots and TikToks.
